## Short Description

Use Google Maps for place search and Apple Maps for navigation with one iOS Shortcut.

## Longer Description

Google Maps is excellent for finding places, reviews, and business details. Apple Maps is deeply integrated with iPhone, CarPlay, Siri, and Apple Watch. This shortcut lets you share a place from Google Maps and open Apple Maps directions immediately.
